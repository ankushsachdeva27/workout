PRE-WORKOUT ROUTINE
Start 15–20 min before lifting
1
Light cardio warm-up
Brisk walk, spot jog or cycling. Gets blood flowing, raises core temperature, protects joints. Never skip at 37 — cold muscles tear.
5 min
2
Arm circles + shoulder rolls
10 forward + 10 backward arm circles. 10 shoulder rolls each direction. Opens rotator cuff before any pressing movement.
1 min
3
Hip circles + leg swings
10 circles each side. 10 front-back leg swings per leg. Critical on Day 3 before squats and lunges. Activates glutes and hip flexors.
1 min
4
Wrist rotations (forearm days)
20 circles each direction. Warms up wrist joints before barbell wrist curls and reverse curls. Prevents forearm strain.
30 sec
5
Warm-up set on first exercise
1 set at 40–50% working weight for 12–15 reps. Never jump straight to heavy. Mandatory — protects tendons and joints.
1 set
★
Pre-workout meal (60 min before)
Banana + nuts, 2 rotis + dal, or oats with milk. You need carbs for energy. Don't train on an empty stomach.
60 min pre

HYDRATION + MINDSET
500 ml water 30 min before training. Dehydration cuts strength output by up to 15%.
Check last session's log before starting. Your only goal: beat last week by 1 rep.
Forearm exercises: use lighter weight than you think. Forearms overtrain quickly — form over load always.

POST-WORKOUT ROUTINE
Do within 30 min of finishing
1
Cool-down walk
3–5 min slow walk. Brings heart rate down gradually. Prevents blood pooling in legs. Don't stop abruptly after heavy sets.
3–5 min
2
Static stretches (day-specific)
D1: Doorframe chest + shoulder stretch 30 sec each. D2: Child's pose + lat stretch + forearm stretch. D3: Quad + hip flexor stretch 40 sec each leg + calf stretch.
2–3 min
3
Forearm stretch (after Day 2)
Extend arm forward, fingers up, pull back gently with other hand 30 sec. Then fingers down, pull back 30 sec. Critical to prevent tennis elbow.
1 min
4
Post-workout meal (within 45 min)
Most important meal. 35–40g protein + fast carbs. Options: 4 eggs + 2 rotis · 200g paneer + rice · curd + chapati + dal · whey + banana.
Within 45 min
5
Log your session
Write exercise → weight → sets → reps. Set next target. You cannot improve what you don't track. This is the #1 factor in long-term progress.
5 min
😴
RECOVERY RULES
Sleep 7–8 hours
80% of muscle is built during deep sleep. Growth hormone peaks in first 2 hours. Non-negotiable at 37.
Protein spread all day
145–160g across 4–5 meals. Absorption is capped at ~40g per meal — don't eat it all at once.
3–4 litres water daily
Muscles are 75% water. Dehydration = slower recovery, more soreness, less strength output next day.
Active rest days
Light 20 min walk + full body stretch. Active recovery accelerates muscle repair by increasing blood flow.

⚡ GOLDEN RULES — EVERY SESSION

Warm up 5–7 min before every session — never skip at 37
Do 1 warm-up set at 50% weight before each compound exercise
Compound (★): 2 min rest · Isolation: 60 sec · Machine: 60–90 sec
Progressive overload: beat last week by 1 rep before adding weight
Protein: 145–160g/day — eggs, paneer, dal, chicken, curd
Drink water between every single set — 3–4 litres daily
Sleep 7–8 hours — muscle is built during sleep, not in the gym
Track every workout: sets, reps, weight — write it always
Post-workout meal within 45 min — 35–40g protein + carbs
Forearms: lighter weight, higher reps, never rush — they overtrain fast
